NASA L'SPACE Mission Concept Academy

Lead Systems Engineer (Summer 2023)

Took the role of lead systems engineer to conduct the preliminary design of a life exploration rover, with a scientific focus on the Ocrator crater of the drawf planet Ceres.

I loved working with this team, 15 students from different universities across the nation. Naturally, the project was completely remote. It was challenging to work online; but eventually, the team got used to it.

In this program, we had to pass 4 reports, including Mission Concept Review (MCR), System Requirements Review (SRR), Mission Definition Review (MDR), and Preliminary Design Review (PDR) These reports are available upon request.

Portal Sentry Turret Design

Personal project (Summer 2023)

I recreated the design of the Sentry Turret from the Portal franchise, focusing on design similarity and mechanical features. In particular, I prototyped an internal mechanism to rotate and extended the sentry's wings, allowing for movement and rotation.

I started this project due to the amusing idea of having an stationary Sentry Turret in my room. Therefore, my goal is to 3D print this model and make it functional
